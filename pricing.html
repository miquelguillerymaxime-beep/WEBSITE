Why query-based

You pay for what the agents use.

Seats measure people. Agents don't map to people. Pricing that scales on queries means a solo dev with one heavy agent and a team with a fleet each pay for the work they actually do.

Your Community license never expires. We only ever pause paid licenses for non-payment, never the free tier. Start free, stay free as long as you like.

Self-hosting

Code that never leaves.

Memtrace runs deterministically and locally. No model reads your source to build the graph, so on Enterprise the whole stack can run inside your own environment.

Built for regulated and complex codebases. Sovereignty is not an add-on, it is how the engine is designed. Talk to us about a self-hosted deployment.
